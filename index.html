About Dendrons.ai

Dendrons.ai builds AI products that help organisations act on the right signals - from the media, from their stakeholders, and from the people actively looking for them. Our products are informed by real-world experience across government, financial services, healthcare, education, and consumer sectors.

Dendrons.ai is supported by Google Cloud for Startups and is an Anthropic Claude Partner.

Our Founder

Dendrons.ai was founded by Nivedita Pandey, an AI and Digital Innovation Leader with a Wharton MBA (Joseph Wharton Scholar) and a distinction in Computer Science Engineering, with 16 years of experience building digital products, platforms, and businesses across US, UK, and Asian markets.

Nivedita's global career spans launching new physical and digital products at Estée Lauder in New York, working with MNCs and startups in London, launching India's first digital gold product, and building and scaling an Asia-wide multimillion-dollar Private Capital business vertical at Microsoft in Singapore.

Her expertise has been leveraged by corporates (Microsoft, HSBC, EY, Estée Lauder), by governments and multilaterals (World Bank, Reserve Bank Innovation Hub, India's Central and State governments), and by startups backed by leading VCs.

Her work has been recognised globally - at UN Women New York (top 10 of ~450 global pitches for innovative tech solution), at British Telecom London (1st prize for most innovative pitch), by the State Chief Secretary in India (for her leadership and execution excellence in DBT as Prime Minister's Fellow), at Microsoft Singapore (APAC Accelerator Award), and by the Singapore government as a high-calibre innovator and entrepreneur.

Startups founded by her in the past have been incubated at Penn Incubator (backed by National Science Foundation, USA) and Antler Singapore (top 3% of global applicants).
