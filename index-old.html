About Dendrons AI

Dendrons AI builds responsible AI products for organizations operating in high-trust, regulated environments. Our products are informed by real-world experience across government, financial services, healthcare, education, and retail sectors.

Our Founder

Dendrons AI was founded by Ms. Nivedita Pandey, an Ivy League-trained digital innovation leader with 16 years of experience building digital products, platforms, and businesses across US, UK, and Asian markets. Her entire career has focused on launching new-to-market, category-creating products and businesses.

As a Prime Minister's Fellow, she led one of India's first DBT pilots and scaled the implementation to rank first nationwide, earning recognition from senior government leaders. Her career includes launching new physical and digital products at Estée Lauder in New York, working with MNCs and startups in London, launching and scaling India's first digital gold product, and building and scaling an Asia-wide multimillion-dollar Private Capital business vertical at Microsoft in Singapore, where she won awards for her work.

Her innovative ideas have been recognized globally - winning first prize at BT London and ranking in the top 10 at UN Women New York. She brings deep strategy and growth expertise across financial services, consumer, enterprise technology, and government sectors.
